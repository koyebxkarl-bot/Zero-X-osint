Responsible Use & Ethics

What Is OSINT?

Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) refers to the collection and analysis of information that is lawfully available from public sources. OSINT is commonly used in cybersecurity, journalism, research, and education.

Acceptable Use

  • Educational and research purposes
  • Learning about data exposure and digital footprints
  • Security awareness and self-assessment
  • Ethical investigations with proper authorization

Unacceptable Use

  • Harassment, stalking, or intimidation
  • Doxxing or targeting private individuals
  • Impersonation or social engineering
  • Blackmail, extortion, or threats
  • Any activity that violates privacy or law

User Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that their actions comply with all applicable local, national, and international laws. The website operator does not encourage or endorse misuse.

Ethical Commitment

This project promotes awareness, responsible use, and ethical understanding of publicly available information. Abuse or misuse may result in restricted access to related services.
